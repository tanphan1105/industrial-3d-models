Technical FAQ

Common Questions

What file formats are included?

Most models are available in widely used formats such as OBJ, FBX and GLB, making them suitable for 3D software, web viewers and AR workflows.

Are the models built to real-world scale?

Yes. The models are prepared at real-world scale so they are easier to use in technical presentations, product visualization and spatial review.

Can I use these models for commercial projects and marketing?

Yes. When purchased through Fab or CGTrader, the models come with a Standard Royalty-Free License, allowing you to use them safely in commercial renders, video marketing, product presentations, and AR applications.

Do you offer custom models based on CAD or P&ID drawings?

Yes. If you need a specific RO skid, vessel or industrial component that is not listed in the store, you can request a custom 3D model based on your drawings or references.

Which models are suitable for AR or web viewing?

AR-ready and GLB-based assets are the best fit for interactive product demos, client presentations and browser-based 3D viewing.

Who are these models for?

They are built for sales engineers, equipment suppliers, 3D artists, technical marketers and teams that need to present industrial water treatment equipment clearly.

Are these models created from real engineering data?

Yes, these models originate from engineering-grade CAD workflows to ensure precise dimensions, exact proportions, and mechanical accuracy. They are designed to meet the strict standards required for industrial and B2B visualization.

Are they native parametric CAD files (like STEP or IGES)?

No. To ensure maximum compatibility for sales teams and 3D artists, the original engineering data has been meticulously converted and optimized into high-quality polygonal meshes (OBJ, FBX, GLB). This gives you CAD-level visual accuracy without requiring heavy engineering software to view or render them.

Is the geometry optimized for professional rendering?

Absolutely. The models feature clean, well-structured topology (primarily quads) that strikes the perfect balance: providing excellent visual fidelity for close-up rendering while remaining efficient enough for real-time web viewers and AR presentations.

Can I customize the materials and modify the components?

Yes. The models maintain a professional hierarchy where components like frames, pressure vessels, pipes, and valves are separated as individual objects. Materials are cleanly assigned by component, making it incredibly easy to apply your own PBR textures or hide specific parts.

Do the models include internal components like filter media?

Most models focus on high-detail exterior accuracy for system-level visualization. However, where applicable (such as transparent housings), internal elements like filter cartridges are included. Please check the specific product description for details on internal modeling.
